First Classification Society in Arab World: Tasneef Maritime is leading and the first Classification Society in all Arab world with Head Office located in the UAE by serving in a cost effective and efficient manner to ensure quality in all parts of our delivery.

Tasneef Maritime provide Classification and statutory services and assistance to the maritime industry and regulatory bodies as regards maritime safety and pollution prevention, based on the accumulation of maritime knowledge and technology.

The main objective of Tasneef Maritime is to verify the structural strength and integrity of essential parts of the ship’s hull and its appendages, and the reliability and function of the propulsion and steering systems, power generation and those other features and auxiliary systems which have been built into the ship in order to maintain essential services on board for the purpose of safe operation of a ship. Tasneef Maritime safely manage ships to their full potential, maintain operational effectiveness and safe guards’ life, preserve environment and protect assets. We work closely with all our stakeholders to achieve these mutually beneficial goals. Classification of ships achieves highest degree of quality in design and construction.

Tasneef Maritime aim to achieve this objective through the development and application of their own rules and by verifying compliance with international and/or national statutory regulations on behalf of flag Administrations. Tasneef Maritime offers vide range of services in Middle East and around the world to our various clients and in number of sectors through our experienced surveyors and expert in field of shipping. In Close co-operation with the worlds maritime industries and international organizations (IACS & IMO) and stake holders, our research and development programme & technical studies with high level of expertise in safety and quality contributes to development of new rules and marine innovations to promote the highest standards in ship safety and the prevention of marine pollution. In short span of time, Tasneef Maritime’s reputation and trustworthiness achieved through excellence in all walk of marine programme has put in for front of marine classification business.
